FAQ

Everything You
Need to Know

What does "permanent" actually mean?+
Permanent means your pixel will never be deleted, moved, or reassigned. It is cryptographically recorded on our distributed infrastructure with redundancy across multiple global data centers. We also hold a legal preservation guarantee — your pixel coordinates and content are contractually protected in perpetuity.
What happens if Zepixal shuts down?+
We've taken this seriously. All pixel data is mirrored to a decentralized network and stored in an escrow vault with an independent foundation. Even if Zepixal as a company ceases to operate, a legal and technical structure ensures the wall persists for at least 50 years.
Can I update my pixel after purchasing?+
Basic pixels can be updated once per year. Premium and Business pixels can be updated unlimited times. The update history is preserved, so viewers can explore how your pixel evolved over time.
How does the Time Capsule work?+
When you create a time capsule, your content is encrypted and sealed until the date you choose. On that date, it automatically unlocks — publicly or privately, based on your preference. You can set capsules for any date up to the year 2100.
Is my content secure and private?+
All content is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256. Private pixels are only visible to you and people you authorize. We never sell or share your data. You retain full copyright ownership of everything you upload.
Can I gift a pixel to someone?+
Absolutely. Pixels make incredible gifts for birthdays, anniversaries, memorials, or just because. You can purchase a pixel and transfer ownership to any email address — they'll receive a beautiful gift notification with instructions to customize their pixel.
